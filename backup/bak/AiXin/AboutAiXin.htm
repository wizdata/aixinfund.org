History:
The recent HIV/AIDS epidemic in Asia, in particular, China, triggered the formation of the AiXin Foundation. AiXin is a non-profit, non-political, public supported charitable organization founded in 2002 by a group of overseas Chinese American.

Mission:
AiXin is dedicated to improve basic health care through education, and provide humanitarian assistance in the developing countries. Currently, its goal is to promote the HIV/AIDS awareness and prevention in China.

Objectives:
The Foundation aims to initiate its programs first in China:
  • To promote basic health care education programs in the impoverished areas of China,
  • To support local communities to initiate and participate in AIDS prevention/care programs,
  • To work with other non-profit organizations, foundations, and health/education institutes to enhance the healthy living environment in the Pacific Rim.
