To Live:  Let’s Fight AIDS Together!

TV Special  -- HIV/AIDS Awareness in China:

How serious is the HIV/AIDS pandemic situation in China?  What progress has been made on HIV prevention and education?  How do the HIV orphans survive?  What kind of hardship are they facing?  What can the overseas Chinese do for them?  If you want to know more about these issues, please tune-in TV special Call-In program on Montgomery county Comcast Channel 13.

Purposes: 

  • To provide current HIV/AIDS information to overseas Chinese 
  • To show our care for these orphans, echoing the recent Chinese TV programs  

Date and Time:  1/16/04 (Friday), 8:00 to 10:00 pm

Sponsored by: AiXin Foundation and World Today TV

Hosted by: Mr. David Huang, World Today TV on Comcast Channel 13

Program: 

  • A short preview of HIV/AIDS documentary produced by AiXin
  • Prof. Cao Yunzhen - an overview on HIV/AIDS in China
  • China Central Television (CCTV) program ‘Face to Face’ – Interview with Dr. Gao Yaojie
  • Special message from Dr. Gao Yaojie to our audience in US
  • Live on line interview with Mr. Hu Jia from Beijing, China
  • Ms. Geng Qian - information sharing on *PATH’s involvement of HIV/AIDS in China
  • Dr. Weng Yongkai - information sharing on AiXin Foundation’s projects
  • Audience call-in, after each section of the program

 

About our Guests:

- Prof. Cao Yunzhen is currently working with Dr. David Ho at Aaron Diamond AIDS Research Center.  She is an    outstanding expert in the field of HIV/AIDS research and clinical treatment, received numerous awards.

- Dr. Gao Yaojie is a world known figure in treating, helping and advocating for many AIDS victims in Henan province in China.  Her heroic work is still ongoing today.

- Mr. Hu Jia is a young, capable and committed volunteer currently helping the orphans and their HIV infected family in China. He is actively involved in many environmental and community concerns.  Many Chinese and international TV and news media have interviewed him on these topics.

- Ms. Geng Qian is working as a senior program officer for PATH (*Program for Appropriate Technology in Health, an international non-government organization based in US).  She has extensive experience in working collaboratively on different aspects of HIV/AIDS in developing countries.

- Dr. Weng Yongkai is the founder and president of AiXin Foundation.  

This is a call-in program.  Please be an active participant – Tell your friends and relatives to tune-in (Comcast Channel 13) and call-in.  The orphans need your attention!
