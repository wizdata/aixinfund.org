How to finance a rural student

1.       Download a donation form from Aixin Foundation’s website: www.AixinFund.org

If it’s inconvenient to get on the internet, you can send a check directly to address listed below, and indicate what your donation is for.

2.       Send your donation form and check to

Aixin Foundation, Inc.

13621 Valley Oak Circle,

Rockville, MD 20850

USA

3.       Aixin Foundation will send back to you the thank latter (as a tax credit receipt)

4.       Every fall, Aixin volunteers responsible for education assistant program will distribute poor students to you. You will receive application forms, transcripts, and signs of assisted students after they get the assistantship.

5.       If Aixin volunteers have a home visit to your assisted students, you will receive photos of students, their schools and families, and get additional information.

6.       If you are willing to correspond to assisted students, please give an indication on your donation. Aixin staffs will mail to you to explain how to correspond with students. Also, we will help to forward your letter to your assisted students.
