DengFeng County, Henan - visit Dr. Gao Yaojie
Aixin members visit Dr. Gao Yaojie, who is known as the first person to recognize the threat of AIDS in China and work in spreading public awareness and education of AIDS. Aixin President Yongkai Weng presents Dr. Gao with a copy of Aixin's book "AIDS in China." Dr. Gao explains how she discovers and exposes illegal underground blood selling activities and how she finds and provides assistance to rural villages with high AIDS infection rates.
